Our Legacy

Crafted by Hand, Built to Last

Operating directly out of the historic manufacturing hub of Amritsar, Punjab, S.K. KARA stands as a benchmark of genuine multi-generational iron craft.

Under the visionary guidance of Prop. Kailash Chander and our factory elders, we do not mass-produce generic steel trinkets. We manufacture authentic, heavy iron (Loha) Karas built with high-grade strength, traditional physical weight balance, and a brilliant premium finish.
